My Story

I joined an organization called CoderSpace in 2015 as a high school junior. I received an email from my principal about it , and my mom made me apply. A fond story I enjoy sharing is how CoderSpace's co-founder, Mr. Demond, waitlisted me because I told him I didn't like working in teams. I later called him and asked if I wasn't getting in, and he decided to let me join CoderSpace.

During my time there, I learned front-end languages (HTML, CSS, and Javascript) and made one for a locally black-owned business. I was voted for team leader by my teammates and learned what leadership is all about that summer. The following summers of 2016 and 2017, I returned to CoderSpace as a teaching assistant. I was promoted as an instructor in the 2018 summer (which later turned into a project management job), and decided to finally acknowledge my interest in technology.

I am currently studying technology and hope to learn more languages to decide which field(s) in tech I enjoy!
